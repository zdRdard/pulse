About The Project

The Pulse project began in 2025 with a simple yet ambitious goal: to create a humanoid robot that could truly understand and adapt to human needs and environments.

Our team of engineers, designers, and AI specialists has worked tirelessly to develop a robot that doesn't just perform tasks, but learns, adapts, and anticipates needs in a natural, human-like way.

What sets Pulse apart is our commitment to creating technology that enhances human life without replacing human connection.
